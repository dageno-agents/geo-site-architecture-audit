# Ecommerce Playbook

## Buyer Questions

- Which product is best for my need?
- What size, variant, or bundle should I choose?
- Is it safe, authentic, or durable?
- How does it compare with alternatives?
- What is the return, warranty, and shipping policy?
- What do reviews say?

## High-Value Site Assets

- category pages
- product detail pages
- comparison and buying guides
- review and UGC pages
- shipping, returns, warranty, and support pages
- brand story and trust proof
- FAQ and size/fit guides

## Common Gaps

- category pages are keyword-driven but not decision-driven
- product pages lack comparison or best-fit guidance
- warranty/return policy is detached from product pages
- reviews are present but not summarized into useful buyer evidence
