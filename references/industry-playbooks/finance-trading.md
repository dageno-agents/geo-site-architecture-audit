# Finance And Trading Playbook

## Buyer Questions

- Is this provider regulated and reliable?
- Which account type fits my trading style?
- What are the spreads, commissions, funding fees, and withdrawal rules?
- Which platform or app should I use?
- What products can I trade?
- What are the risks and regional restrictions?

## High-Value Site Assets

- account comparison page
- fees, spreads, commissions, swap/funding explanations
- deposit and withdrawal page
- trust/safety/regulation center
- product market pages
- platform/app/tool pages
- help center and complaint policy
- legal documents and risk disclosures

## Common Gaps

- cost information exists but total trading cost is unclear
- account pages are not connected to product pages
- risk and regulation evidence is scattered
- community concerns such as withdrawals or platform reliability are not answered officially
- regional/language pages have weaker content than the main site

## Safety

Do not promise profits, trading outcomes, or guaranteed AI recommendations.
