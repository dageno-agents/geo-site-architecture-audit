# Local Services Playbook

## Buyer Questions

- Is this provider available near me?
- How much does the service usually cost?
- Can I trust them in my home/business?
- What services are included?
- How soon can I book?
- What reviews or credentials prove reliability?

## High-Value Site Assets

- service pages
- city/location pages
- pricing or estimate pages
- booking/contact pages
- reviews and case studies
- license/insurance/credential pages
- FAQ and service-area pages

## Common Gaps

- pages list services but do not answer pricing or booking questions
- location pages are thin or duplicated
- reviews are not tied to specific services
- credentials and insurance proof are not easy to cite
