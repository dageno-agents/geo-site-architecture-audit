# Regulated Industries

Use this reference for finance, trading, investment, insurance, healthcare, medical, legal, immigration, tax, and other high-trust categories.

## Rules

- Do not imply guaranteed financial, medical, legal, or compliance outcomes.
- Treat AI recommendation as visibility and evidence quality, not guaranteed conversion.
- Treat community posts as reputation evidence, not compliance proof.
- Use official pages, regulators, legal documents, policies, and help centers as primary trust evidence.

## Common GEO Trust Assets

- Trust Center or Safety Hub
- Regulation / license / certification page
- Legal documents and policy pages
- Risk disclosure page
- Complaint or dispute resolution page
- Pricing, fees, and total-cost explanations
- Suitability and limitation sections
- Region and eligibility restrictions
- FAQ for sensitive buyer objections

## Common Missing Modules

- "Is this safe/reliable?" answer block
- "Who is this suitable for?" suitability block
- total-cost examples
- risks and limitations
- region-specific legal status
- comparison with alternatives using neutral criteria

## Executive Framing

```text
In this category, GEO is not just about being mentioned. AI systems need enough trustworthy evidence to decide whether they can responsibly recommend the brand. That means pricing, risk, compliance, suitability, limitations, and support evidence must be easy to cite.
```
