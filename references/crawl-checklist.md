# Crawl Checklist

Use this checklist to plan evidence collection.

## Core Targets

- Homepage
- Primary navigation
- Footer navigation
- Sitemap XML or HTML sitemap
- Robots.txt
- Language selector
- Region selector
- Canonical and hreflang signals

## High-Value Page Types

- Product or service pages
- Pricing, plans, accounts, fees, or packages
- Feature and platform pages
- Solution, use-case, persona, or industry pages
- Comparison, alternatives, or "vs" pages
- Demo, trial, quote, account opening, booking, checkout, or contact pages
- FAQ and help center pages
- Documentation, API, setup, integration, or download pages
- About, trust, security, compliance, legal, privacy, terms, complaint, review, case, and testimonial pages
- Blog, academy, guide, glossary, webinar, report, or resource pages

## Evidence Fields

For each important page, capture:

- URL
- page title
- main heading
- page type
- target user or scenario
- facts/prices/plans/products mentioned
- CTAs
- FAQ presence
- trust/compliance proof
- links to related pages
- apparent GEO role
- weaknesses or missing modules

## Crawl Quality Warnings

Flag these issues:

- redirected regional/language path
- blocked by Cloudflare or bot checks
- JS-only navigation
- thin content
- duplicated generic content across pages
- inconsistent canonical or hreflang
- important pages missing in target language
- help center on separate subdomain with weak internal links
