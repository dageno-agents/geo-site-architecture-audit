# P0/P1/P2 Scoring Rubric

Use this public rubric to assign priority. Do not include proprietary weights in public outputs.

## Criteria

| Criterion | High-Signal Meaning |
|---|---|
| Buyer criticality | Directly affects purchase, signup, account opening, booking, demo, or consultation |
| AI citation value | Answers price, fit, safety, comparison, setup, risk, or "how to start" questions |
| Trust/risk sensitivity | Important for finance, healthcare, legal, insurance, enterprise security, or high-ticket decisions |
| Existing asset leverage | The brand already has relevant pages, but they are fragmented or weakly structured |
| Competitive gap | Competitors or third-party sources answer the question better than the official site |
| Localization gap | Target region/language lacks critical decision pages |

## Priority Rules

### P0

Use for pages/modules that directly affect AI trust, recommendation, pricing, or conversion.

Examples:

- pricing/account/plan comparison
- total cost explanation
- trust center, safety hub, compliance proof
- conversion flow FAQ
- region eligibility, risk disclosure, legal limitation

### P1

Use for product, use-case, platform, integration, and scenario pages that expand recommendation coverage.

Examples:

- product-to-plan matching
- platform/tool comparison
- industry/use-case pages
- implementation/setup decision pages
- competitor or alternative comparison pages

### P2

Use for education, blog, academy, glossary, market/news, and resource pages that support early discovery or topical authority.

Examples:

- glossary definitions
- beginner guides
- market analysis templates
- webinars and resource hubs
- article-to-conversion next-step modules
