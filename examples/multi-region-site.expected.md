# Multi-Region Site Expected Output Sketch

## Current Situation

The brand has multiple regional or language paths. Some buyer-critical pages may exist in the primary language but not in the target region/language.

## GEO Problem

AI may find the global site but fail to answer local buyer questions if pricing, eligibility, support, trust, and legal content is inconsistent across regions.

## P0 Recommendations

| Priority | Buyer Question | Gap Type | Recommendation | Confidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| P0 | Is this available in my region? | unlocalized | Audit region routing, canonical, hreflang, and local eligibility pages. | medium |
| P0 | Which support/legal rules apply locally? | fragmented | Link local support, legal, pricing, and conversion pages from regional navigation. | medium |
